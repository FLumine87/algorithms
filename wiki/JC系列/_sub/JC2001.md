5、软件过程模型
--了解并能够简要解释常见的软件过程模型，如瀑布模型、增量模型、螺旋模型和敏捷模型。
--特别注意螺旋模型的特点：它结合了迭代开发和系统化的风险管理。
- **瀑布模型(Waterfall Model)** - 线性流水式: 线性顺序模型,阶段划分明确(需求->设计->编码->测试->交付),文档驱动,适合**需求稳定的项目**   A linear, sequential approach where each phase (requirements, design, implementation, testing, maintenance) must be completed before the next begins.
- _Advantage:_ Simple to understand and manage, provides clear milestones. 
- _Disadvantage:_ Inflexible to changes, working software is delivered very late in the lifecycle.
- **增量模型(Incremental Development Model)** - 循环累加式: 将软件拆分为多个可独立交付的增量, 逐步开发交付, 每次增量包含**完整的需求分析**,设计,编码和测试环节, 可较早交付可用功能;The system is built and delivered in small, functional increments, with each increment adding new functionality to the previous one.
- _Advantage:_ Users get working software earlier, and risks can be managed by prioritizing high-risk increments. 
- _Disadvantage:_ Requires a robust initial architecture; poor planning can lead to integration issues in later increments.
- **螺旋模型(Spiral Model)** - 风险驱动式：融合迭代开发与系统化风险管理, 每个循环包含制定计划,风险分析,开发,评审步骤,**适合大型高风险项目**An iterative model that combines prototyping and systematic risk analysis in each cycle. Progress is represented as a spiral.
- _Advantage:_ Explicitly addresses risks in each cycle, allowing for mid-course corrections. 
- _Disadvantage:_ Can be complex, expensive, and requires significant risk assessment expertise.
- **敏捷模型(Agile Model)** - 灵活响应式: 以用户需求进化为核心,迭代渐进式开发,强调快速响应变化和持续交付价值,**轻文档重事件**An iterative and incremental approach that emphasizes flexibility, customer collaboration, and delivering working software frequently.
- _Advantage:_ Highly adaptable to change, provides early and continuous delivery of value. 
- _Disadvantage:_ Can be difficult to predict final cost and timeline, requires high customer/ stakeholder involvement.

6、功能性需求与非功能性需求
--能够明确区分两者。功能性需求描述系统应该“做什么”（如用户认证、生成发票）。
--非功能性需求描述系统“做得怎么样”的质量属性（如系统响应时间、安全性）。
1. **Category: Performance** - The system shall load menu pages and confirm orders within 2 seconds for 95% of requests under normal load.
2. **Category: Security** - The system shall encrypt all user information anddata.
3. **Category: Usability** - The system interface shall be intuitive enough for new user.

9、风险管理与项目管理
--在风险识别阶段，能够列举出软件开发中常见的风险类型（如技术风险、人员风险、需求风险、项目风险等）。
1,**Estimation risks**: The initial estimates for project duration and effort may be overly optimistic(re-estimate regularly as the project progresses)

2,**Organizational risks**:A company reorganization or shift in strategic priorities

3,**People risks**:A key senior developer with critical system knowledge may leave the project unexpectedly.(  Implement a knowledge-sharing strategy)

4,**Requirements risks**:  ambiguous, unstable, or incomplete requirements that cause rework(Implement a formal change control process.)

5,**Technology risks**: The development team lacks experience with a new, complex framework or architecture(Conduct a proof-of-concept integration early in the project)

6,**Tools risks**:The tools have a steep learning curve or may be incompatible with existing workflows(Conduct a pilot evaluation before full rollout)

--理解软件项目管理的关键目标（如按时、按预算交付高质量产品）和核心活动（如规划、调度、监控）。
- **Key Objectives:** To deliver a high-quality software product **on time**, **within budget**, and that **meets the specified requirements**.
- **Core Activities:**
a) **Planning:** Defining project scope, objectives, tasks, schedules, resources, and budget.
b) **Scheduling & Estimation:** Breaking down tasks, estimating effort and duration, and creating timelines (e.g., using Gantt charts).
c) **Monitoring & Control:** Tracking project progress against the plan, managing risks, handling changes, and ensuring the project stays on track.
②核心活动:

1,项目规划 Project planning
2,任务管理 Task management
3,资源分配 Resource allocation
4,时间跟踪Time tracking
5,协作工具 Collaboration tools
6,风险管理 Risk management
7,报告与分析 Reporting and analytics
8,预算管理 Budget management

bb) **Execution**:  Implements the project plan by coordinating team members to complete tasks (e.g., Java coding, UI design), managing resource usage, and maintaining regular communication (e.g., daily standups) to ensure smooth progress.
