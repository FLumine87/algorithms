
|函数|语法|功能描述|
|---|---|---|
|`malloc`|`void* malloc(size_t size);`|分配未初始化的内存|
|`calloc`|`void* calloc(size_t num, size_t size);`|分配并初始化为0的内存|
|`realloc`|`void* realloc(void* ptr, size_t new_size);`|重新分配内存|
|`free`|`void free(void* ptr);`|释放内存|
|`atoi`|`int atoi(const char* str);`|字符串转整数|
|`atof`|`double atof(const char* str);`|字符串转浮点数|
|`atol`|`long atol(const char* str);`|字符串转长整型|
|`itoa`|`char* itoa(int value, char* str, int base);`|整数转字符串|
